About Me

Hi, I’m Seth — a recent graduate from the Isenberg School of Management at UMass Amherst, where I studied Marketing and earned a certificate in Data Analytics. I’ve always been curious about how people make decisions and how data can help tell better stories.

I’ve worked in fast-moving environments where showing up, solving problems, and getting results mattered. Whether I was leading a team project, talking to customers, or figuring out a better way to get something done, I leaned into the challenge and learned a lot along the way.
